R in Insurance Conference 2013

This repository contains the presentation and R files of the first R in Insurance conference held at Cass Business School London, 15 July 2015. You can download all files in a .tar.gz or .zip archive format via the links above, or individually from the GitHub repository.

The 2013 R in Insurance conference builds upon the success of the R in Finance and R/Rmetrics events.

The organisers, Markus Gesmann and Andreas Tsanakas, gratefully acknowledge the sponsorship of Mango Solutions and CYBAEA.
